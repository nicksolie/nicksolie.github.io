About Me

For the past year and a half, I’ve worked as an assistant for a real estate appraisal company, and while I’ve enjoyed the independence and responsibly of working on a small team, I’ve decided I want to pursue a career in software engineering. I’m regularly amazed by the scope of work my friends in the field engage in. From the design process, to implementing and deploying their code, I’m astounded by how the tools they create go on to impact others lives. One of my primary goals in the future after GA is to develop my own educational applications from the ground up. I aim to bring my passion for learning and collaboration into my new career and I am excited for what the future holds.
